Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Sep 14, 2024

1. Introduction

I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy. This policy describes how I collect, use, and share information when you use the SimplePod app.

2. Information I Collect

I collect limited information to improve your experience with SimplePod:

  • Podcast RSS Feeds: SimplePod allows you to add RSS feed URLs of your favorite podcasts. This information is stored locally on your device and is not shared with any third party.
  • Usage Data: I may collect non-identifiable information related to your app usage, such as app preferences or errors, to improve app functionality and user experience. This data is anonymized and cannot be linked back to you.

3. How I Use Your Information

The information I collect is used solely to:

  • Provide and improve SimplePod's features.
  • Troubleshoot and fix app-related issues.
  • Enable you to listen to and manage your favorite podcasts.

4. Third-Party Content

SimplePod provides access to third-party podcast content via RSS feeds. I do not control, modify, or host this content. By using SimplePod, you agree to respect the rights of the podcast content creators as governed by their terms of service and privacy policies.

5. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: SimplePod stores your RSS feed URLs and preferences on your device only. I do not collect or store any personal data on any server.

Data Security: While I strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. SimplePod takes reasonable measures to ensure your information is secure.

6. Third-Party Services

SimplePod does not directly use third-party services or analytics that collect personal data. However, some podcasts may use tracking mechanisms as part of their RSS feeds, and you are encouraged to review the privacy policies of the podcasts you listen to.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Effective Date" at the top will be updated.
